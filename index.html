About Me

My name is Caren Jeruto, and I am passionate about technology and its power to create positive change. I have a strong interest in digital innovation and using tech-based solutions to address real-life challenges. My journey in tech is driven by a desire to bridge gaps, especially in communities where access to information and opportunity is limited.

I've worked with startups and community projects to create impactful digital tools—like the BilaWoga emergency alert app and the Shield Maidens platform fighting gender-based violence online. My approach blends technical skills with a human-centered purpose.

Beyond coding, I contribute to digital literacy and empowerment programs, speak on tech inclusion, and mentor young girls in STEM.
